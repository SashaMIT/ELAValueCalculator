  1. Bitcoin Mining Rewards:
    • Block Reward: 3.125 BTC (every 10 minutes)
    • Blocks per Year: 52,560 (6 blocks per hour * 24 hours * 365 days)
    • Annual Mining Rewards (BTC): 164,250 BTC/year (3.125 * 52,560)
  2. Annual Value of Bitcoin's Hashpower:
    • Annual Mining Rewards (USD) = Annual Mining Rewards (BTC) * Bitcoin Price
    • Example: 164,250 BTC * $63,796 = $10.48 billion/year
  3. Elastos' Hashpower Share:
    • Elastos' Share of Hashrate: 45%
    • Annual Value of Elastos' Hashpower = Annual Value of Bitcoin's Hashpower * 45%
    • Example: $10.48 billion * 45% = $4.716 billion

    • Total Supply of ELA (by 2105): 28.22 million tokens
    • Per ELA Token Value = Annual Value of Elastos' Hashpower / Total Supply of ELA

This calculation assumes that the value of ELA is directly proportional to its share of Bitcoin's mining rewards, based on its 45% hashrate contribution through merged mining.
